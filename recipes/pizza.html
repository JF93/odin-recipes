Pizza!

Pizza ... either simply made or very simply ordered.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store bought in this case)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Grated cheese
  • Whatever toppings you desire!

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce over the dough.
  3. Put on all the toppings you want starting with meat, then veggies, then cheese.
  4. Bake the pizza for about 10 to 15 min at about 200 degree Celsius.
  5. Alternativly realize you are to lazy for the previous steps and order pizza instead.
  6. Wait till your food is ready and enjoy
