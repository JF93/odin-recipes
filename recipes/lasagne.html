Lasagne

What kind of dish is lasagne?

Lasagne is a classic pasta dish. If you dont know Lasagne just give it a try.

Ingredients for the bechamel sauce

  • 160 grams butter
  • 160 grams flour
  • 1600 ml milk
  • Pepper
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt

Ingredients for the bolognese

  • 800 grams minced meat
  • Basel
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • 16 table spoons tomato paste
  • 2 cans hacked tomatos
  • 4 small carottes
  • 2 onions
  • Pepper
  • As much garlic as you like
  • Tyme

Additional Ingredients

  1. Olive oil
  2. 400 grams grated cheese
  3. Lasagne sheets

Steps for the bolognese

  1. Dice onions,carots and garlic
  2. Heat oil in a pot and fry the minced meat.
  3. Add onions,carots and garlic and fry some more
  4. Add canned tomatos, tomato paste and all the spices
  5. Let the sauce simmer for 10 minutes

Steps for the bechamel sauce

  1. Melt the butter in a pot
  2. Add flour and immediatly stir
  3. While stirring slowly add milk
  4. Add more milk until you have a thick sauce
  5. Add nutmeg and pepper

Oven preparation

  1. Oil up the backing dish
  2. Add in this order: Pasta, bechamel sauce, bolognese
  3. Repeat step 2 until your backing dish is full and end on bechamel sauce
  4. Add cheese
  5. Backe at 180 degree celsius for about 30 minutes
  6. Enjoy your tasty lasagne just like Garfield would
