Bolo de Caco

What are Bolo de Caco ?

Bolo de Caco is a type of Bread that originate in Portugal that is typically made in a hot pan.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams plain flour.
  • 7 grams yeast.
  • 200 grams sweet potatoes (about 2)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper.
  • 350 millilitres warm water.

Steps

  1. Bake the sweet potatoes in the oven for about 30 to 40 min.
  2. Mash the sweet potatoes
  3. Combine the sweet potatoes and the other ingredients into a soft, wet dough.
  4. Mix the dough for 5 to 10 min.
  5. Cover the dough and take a 30 min break.
  6. Divide the dough into four equal pieces and shape them into flatted rounds.
  7. Take another 30 min break.
  8. Melt a littel butter in a small pan and cook each round over low heat for 8 to 10 min on each side
  9. Wait until cooled of a bit and enjoy.
